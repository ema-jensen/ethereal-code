Hi, I'm a developer that loves clean & simple code.

A little bit about me.

I've made my home base in Eugene, Oregon, with my small growing family. My journey into tech started with a degree in psychology and nutrition. As I started researching directions of where to go with my education, I became intrigued by web development. I wanted to learn how to build and design sites that are user friendly but also esthetically ethereal.

That desire then opened a door that could never be shut. When I learned how to build my first website, I realized I found something that gave me the light & independence I was looking for in my life. Now I've made a full switch to front-end development, where I can use my organization skills and eye for detail to write clean, simple code.

Hand-Coded HTML

My focus is writing clean, well-formatted, semantic HTML5 by hand to make sure that the content is easy to read, easy to collaborate, trouble-shoot and accessible.

Well-Organized CSS

I pride myself on writing CSS that is easy to read and build on. I focus on keeping my CSS lean and fast to load, and I make it a habit to stay up to date on current best practices.

Ease Converting Designs into Code

You can trust me to take a designer's PSD and quickly & accurately convert it into a webpage that is pixel-perfect match.
